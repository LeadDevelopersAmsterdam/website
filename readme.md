# Lead Developers Amsterdam

A cozy meetup group for Lead Developers, Tech Leads, Chapter Leads and Senior Engineers heading towards Lead positions. We talk about:

* Team structures (chapters, guilds, component teams)
* Personal development coaching
* Hiring management
* Engineers onboarding
* Monitoring/measuring/improving productivity in teams
* OPS - infrastructure, CI
* Dealing with non-technical people
* Managing technical debt
* In-house education
